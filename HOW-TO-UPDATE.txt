============================================================
  HOW TO UPDATE THE APP'S DATA
============================================================

There is really just ONE button:

  * "Update App Data"  ->  Double-click it. It's SMART:
       - It checks first.
       - If nothing is new, it says "ALREADY UP TO DATE" in a few
         seconds and stops. (Safe to click anytime - it won't waste
         an hour rebuilding when there's nothing to do.)
       - If new government data exists, it downloads it, rebuilds
         everything, and publishes it live - all by itself.

  (There's also a "Check For New Data" button that only peeks and
   changes nothing, but you don't need it - "Update App Data"
   already checks first.)


------------------------------------------------------------
  THE NORMAL ROUTINE
------------------------------------------------------------

  Whenever you feel like it (about once a month is plenty):
  double-click "Update App Data". That's the whole routine.

       - "ALREADY UP TO DATE" -> great, nothing to do.
       - "DONE - ... published live" -> it found new data and
         updated everything for you.
       - "SETUP NEEDED" -> your key is missing/expired; it opens
         the setup pages (see FIRST-TIME SETUP below).
       - "STOPPED" -> read the message, send it to Claude.
         Nothing gets broken.

  A black window opens while it works. If it found new data it can
  take 30-60 minutes - just leave the window open until it finishes.


------------------------------------------------------------
  FIRST-TIME SETUP (only once, ever)
------------------------------------------------------------

The first time you click "Update App Data", it needs a free "key"
to log in to the government data site. It will open two web pages:

  1. Token page: sign in (or make a free account), create a token
     (choose "Read"), and copy it.
  2. Dataset page: if it asks you to agree to terms, click Agree.

Then paste the copied key into a file named:

     hf_token.txt

...in THIS folder, and save it. (Just the key, nothing else.)

Double-click "Update App Data" again. From now on it just works -
no setup, no typing.


------------------------------------------------------------
  WANT IT ON YOUR DESKTOP?
------------------------------------------------------------

Right-click "Update App Data.cmd" -> Send to -> Desktop (create
shortcut). Rename the icon to whatever you like. Now it's a button
on your desktop.


------------------------------------------------------------
  GOOD TO KNOW
------------------------------------------------------------

  * "Update App Data" is safe to run anytime. If there's no new
    government data, it just rebuilds and republishes what you have.
  * Your key in hf_token.txt stays on your computer and is never
    shared or uploaded.
  * 2024 data from the government is still marked "preliminary," so
    a new release may revise recent months. That's normal.
